About Me

I am currently a Manager - Industry 4.0 Innovation Center at C4i4 Lab, Pune, where I lead a dedicated team working on advanced Computer Vision projects. My research interests encompass Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Computer Vision, with a strong focus on digital transformation and industrial innovation.

Prior to my current role, I embarked on a Ph.D. journey in Microwave Imaging at Savitribai Phule Pune University. My doctoral research involved developing spatially varying regularization techniques for microwave imaging. Before delving into Microwave Imaging, I gained valuable experience working on 3D image reconstruction and EEG data analysis.

I have spearheaded several digital transformation projects aimed at revolutionizing manufacturing processes and enhancing efficiency through innovative software solutions. Notably, I established and managed the iFactory (Model Factory) as a Digital Transformation Platform, training over 2,500+ individuals on Industry 4.0 concepts.
