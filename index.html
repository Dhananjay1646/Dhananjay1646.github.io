About Me

I am a Solution Architect specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Automation, with a proven track record of leading digital transformation initiatives and building scalable AI-driven solutions. At C4i4 Lab’s Industry 4.0 Innovation Center, I manage and mentor cross-functional teams, bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world industrial applications.

My journey spans applied research and development in AI, data science, and computer vision, where I have designed intelligent systems for predictive & preventive maintenance, process optimization, fleet management, and advanced visualization dashboards. I have also spearheaded the setup of the iFactory (Model Factory), a flagship digital transformation platform that has trained and upskilled more than 2,500 professionals in Industry 4.0 technologies.

Before stepping into leadership roles, I worked on microwave imaging, 3D image reconstruction, EEG data analysis, and embedded systems, contributing to academia and industrial innovation alike. My career blends technical depth with strategic vision, enabling me to simplify complex problems into practical, deployable solutions that drive measurable impact.

With a strong foundation in AI/ML, data-driven product development, and software engineering, I remain passionate about exploring emerging technologies and fostering collaboration to shape the future of intelligent systems.
